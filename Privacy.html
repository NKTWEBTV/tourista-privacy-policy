When you use the Tourista app, especially when signing in through Facebook Login, we may collect the following information:

  • Name

  • Location Data (if enabled by the user)

This data is used solely to provide a personalized tour management experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Facebook Login

We use Facebook Login to authenticate users. This allows you to securely access the app using your Facebook account. Tourista only accesses basic information (name, email, and profile picture) and does not post anything on your behalf.

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide the app services or comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
